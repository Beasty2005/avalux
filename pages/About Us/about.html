Who We Are

Avalux General
Trading FZC

Avalux General Trading FZC is an internationally active supplier of premium electrical and lighting solutions, headquartered in Sharjah, UAE. We serve contractors, consultants, developers, and procurement teams across Africa, the Middle East, and Europe.

Our strength lies in a carefully curated product portfolio that spans electrical distribution, industrial and architectural lighting, hazardous-area fixtures, cable systems, and more — all sourced from globally recognised manufacturers.

Whether you're building a high-rise in Dubai, an oil platform in the Gulf, or a commercial complex in London, Avalux delivers the right product, at the right time, with the right support.
